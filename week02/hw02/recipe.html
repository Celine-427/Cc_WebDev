Strawberry Cream Cake



This strawberry cream cake is a sweet and tasty dessert. It has soft cake, fresh strawberries, and light whipped cream.
It is perfect for any event or just as a special treat. Everyone will love how good it tastes and how nice it looks!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Fresh strawberries
  • Whipped cream

Procedure

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C). Allow it to heat for at least 10 minutes

  2. In a large bowl, mix 2 cups of flour, 1 cup of sugar, and 1 tsp of baking powder. Add 1/2 cup of softened butter and blend until crumbly

  3. Add 2 eggs, 1 tsp of vanilla extract, and 1 cup of milk. Stir well until the batter is smooth

  4. Grease a baking pan. Pour the batter into the pan, spreading it evenly

  5. Bake for 30-35 minutes. Check with a toothpick. Let the cake cool completely (20-30 mins)

  6. While cake cools, whip heavy cream until stiff peaks form (or use store-bought whipped cream)

  7. Spread whipped cream over cooled cake

  8. Wash and dry fresh strawberries. Slice or leave whole, then arrange on top

  9. Slice and serve! Enjoy your strawberry cream cake!
